# EUONGELION Press Kit Outline

*What a comprehensive press kit should contain for media, bloggers, and partners.*

---

## Overview

A press kit (also called a media kit) provides journalists, bloggers, podcasters, and potential partners with everything they need to write about or feature EUONGELION. The goal is to make covering us easy - reducing back-and-forth and ensuring accurate representation.

---

## Press Kit Components

### 1. One-Page Summary / Fact Sheet

**Purpose:** Quick reference for busy journalists

**Contents:**
- App name and tagline: "EUONGELION: Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Design"
- One-paragraph description (50-75 words)
- Key differentiators (3-4 bullet points)
- Launch date
- Platforms available (Web, iOS via PWA, Android via PWA)
- Pricing (Free)
- Founder name and brief bio
- Website URL
- App Store / Play Store links (if applicable)
- Press contact email

**Format:** PDF, one page, print-ready

---

### 2. About EUONGELION - Full Description

**Purpose:** Comprehensive background for feature articles

**Contents:**

#### The Problem We Address (150-200 words)
- Devotional apps that prioritize engagement metrics over spiritual formation
- Gamification of faith (streaks, badges, notifications)
- Shallow content designed for quick consumption
- Design that fragments attention rather than focuses it

#### Our Approach (150-200 words)
- Ancient wisdom meets modern design
- Spiritual formation over engagement metrics
- Sacred minimalism in design philosophy
- Scripture in context, not isolated verses
- Tools like Soul Audit for genuine self-examination

#### Key Features (bullet list with brief descriptions)
- Daily Devotionals
- Thematic Series
- Soul Audit
- Offline Reading (PWA)
- Reading Progress
- Bookmarks
- Dark Mode First design

#### Target Audience
- Christians seeking depth in their faith
- New believers building foundation
- Mature believers wanting fresh perspective
- Ministry leaders seeking resources
- Anyone weary of shallow spiritual apps

#### Future Vision
- Shepherd features for pastors and small group leaders
- Expanded series library
- Community features (thoughtfully designed)

**Format:** Word document and PDF, 2-3 pages

---

### 3. Founder Story / Bio

**Purpose:** Humanize the brand, provide interview angles

**Contents:**

#### Short Bio (50 words)
For quick attribution in articles

#### Medium Bio (150 words)
For feature articles and podcast introductions

#### Long Bio / Founder Story (400-600 words)
- Personal faith background
- The frustration that sparked EUONGELION
- Why this problem matters personally
- Vision for impact
- Relevant professional background

#### Founder Photo
- Headshot (professional, warm)
- Casual/working photo
- Multiple resolutions (300dpi for print, 72dpi for web)

**Format:** Separate document with embedded photos; photos also as separate files

---

### 4. App Description Variations

**Purpose:** Ready-to-use descriptions for different contexts

**Contents:**

#### One-Line (10 words)
"A devotional app designed for depth, not distraction."

#### Tweet-Length (280 characters)
"EUONGELION: Ancient wisdom meets modern design. A devotional app that prioritizes your encounter with Scripture over engagement metrics. No gamification. No guilt. Just space to meet God. Free & offline-ready."

#### Short Paragraph (50 words)
For social media, directories, brief mentions

#### Medium Paragraph (100 words)
For app store descriptions, partner websites

#### Full Description (250-300 words)
For feature articles, detailed reviews

**Format:** Single document with all variations

---

### 5. Screenshots and Visuals

**Purpose:** Visual assets for articles, reviews, social sharing

**Contents:**

#### App Screenshots
- Home/Dashboard view
- Daily Devotional reading view
- Series browse/selection view
- Soul Audit interface
- Bookmarks collection
- Settings/preferences
- Offline indicator

**Specifications:**
- High resolution (at least 1290 x 2796 for mobile)
- Both light and dark mode versions
- Clean (no personal data visible)
- Annotated versions available (with feature callouts)

#### Lifestyle Photos
- Person using app on phone (morning setting)
- App on tablet in coffee shop
- Offline use scenario
- Multiple diverse users if possible

#### Device Mockups
- iPhone mockup
- Android phone mockup
- iPad/tablet mockup
- Multiple devices together

**Format:** PNG files (transparent where appropriate), organized in folders by type

---

### 6. Logo and Brand Assets

**Purpose:** Consistent brand representation across media

**Contents:**

#### Primary Logo
- Full color version
- Single color (black) version
- Single color (white) version
- Versions with and without tagline

#### Logo Specifications
- Minimum size requirements
- Clear space requirements
- What NOT to do (stretch, recolor, etc.)

#### Brand Colors
- Primary palette with hex codes
- Secondary palette with hex codes
- Background colors

#### Typography
- Primary font names
- Where fonts can be obtained

#### App Icon
- Full resolution
- Various sizes (1024x1024, 512x512, 256x256, etc.)

**Format:**
- Vector files (SVG, EPS) for logos
- PNG files at multiple resolutions
- Brand guidelines PDF

### 7. Press Release Templates

**Purpose:** Ready-to-distribute announcements

**Contents:**

#### Launch Press Release
- Standard press release format
- Headline options
- Dateline
- Body copy (inverted pyramid style)
- Boilerplate about EUONGELION
- Contact information
- ###

#### Feature Update Press Release Template
- Adaptable for new feature announcements
- Fill-in-the-blank sections

#### Partnership Announcement Template
- For church, ministry, or organizational partnerships

**Format:** Word documents (editable) and PDFs

---

### 8. FAQ for Press

**Purpose:** Anticipate and answer common questions

**Contents:**

#### About the App
- What is EUONGELION? (Etymology: Greek for "good news")
- What makes it different from YouVersion, Dwell, etc.?
- Is it free? What's the business model?
- What denominations is it for?

#### About the Founder
- Who created it and why?
- What's the founder's background?
- Is this a ministry or a business?

#### Technical Questions
- What platforms is it available on?
- Does it work offline?
- Is my data private/secure?

#### Usage Questions
- Do I need an account?
- Can churches/ministries use it?
- Are there group features?

**Format:** Document with expandable sections

---

### 9. Key Messages and Talking Points

**Purpose:** Ensure consistent messaging across interviews

**Contents:**

#### Core Positioning Statement
The single most important thing we want people to understand

#### Three Key Messages
1. [Primary differentiator]
2. [User benefit]
3. [Vision/impact]

#### Supporting Points for Each Message
Specific facts, features, or stories that support each key message

#### Bridge Phrases
How to redirect difficult questions back to key messages

#### What We Don't Say
Messages or framings to avoid

**Format:** Internal document (not for distribution)

---

### 10. Sample Interview Questions

**Purpose:** Help podcasters and interviewers prepare

**Contents:**

#### For General Audiences
- What inspired you to create EUONGELION?
- What's wrong with existing devotional apps?
- What does "ancient wisdom meets modern design" mean?
- Who is EUONGELION for?
- What's the Soul Audit?

#### For Tech/Startup Audiences
- Why build a PWA instead of native apps?
- What's your approach to growth without gamification?
- How do you think about metrics for a spiritual app?

#### For Faith/Ministry Audiences
- How do you hope EUONGELION impacts people spiritually?
- What role does design play in devotional practice?
- How did your own faith journey shape the app?

**Format:** PDF, organized by audience type

---

### 11. Testimonials and Reviews

**Purpose:** Third-party validation

**Contents:**

#### User Testimonials (with permission)
- 5-7 testimonials
- Diverse users (age, background, use case)
- Attribution with permission

#### Press Mentions/Reviews
- Quotes from any coverage
- Publication names and dates
- Links to full articles

#### Notable Users
- Any recognizable names using the app (with permission)

**Format:** Document with pull-quotes formatted for easy copy/paste

---

### 12. Contact Information

**Purpose:** Clear path to reach the right person

**Contents:**

#### Press Contact
- Name
- Email
- Phone (if available)
- Response time expectation

#### General Inquiries
- Email
- Website contact form

#### Social Media
- All platform handles

#### Physical Address
- If applicable/desired

**Format:** Included in every document; also standalone contact sheet
