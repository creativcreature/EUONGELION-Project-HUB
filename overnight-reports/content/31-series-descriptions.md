# EUONGELION Series Descriptions

*Compelling 2-3 sentence descriptions for all planned and existing devotional series*

---

## Existing Series (From Database/Content)

### Finding Your Identity in Christ
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** Beginner

Before you can walk with God, you must know who you are in Him. This series strips away worldly labels and cultural expectations to reveal the eternal truth of your identity as a chosen, forgiven, and beloved child of God.

---

### The Power of Daily Prayer
**Duration:** 14 days | **Level:** Intermediate

Prayer is not a religious obligation; it is the breath of your soul. Over two weeks, discover how to move from reciting words to experiencing genuine conversation with the Father who already knows your heart.

---

### Walking Through Trials
**Duration:** 10 days | **Level:** Intermediate

When darkness surrounds you, Scripture becomes your light. This series meets you in your pain, not with easy answers, but with the presence of a God who walks through the valley with you.

---

### Foundations of Faith
**Duration:** 21 days | **Level:** Beginner

What do Christians actually believe, and why does it matter? This three-week journey through essential doctrines builds a faith that can withstand the storms of doubt, culture, and crisis.

---

## Substack Series (From Content Archive)

### Too Busy for God
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** When Your Schedule Becomes Your Savior

In a world that worships productivity, choosing stillness is an act of holy rebellion. Through the wisdom literature of Ecclesiastes and Proverbs, discover what you are too busy doing that keeps you from the One who gave you life.

---

### Hearing God in the Noise
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Finding Divine Voice in Digital Chaos

God is not silent; we are simply too loud to hear. This series quiets the inner and outer noise to help you discern the still, small voice that has been speaking all along.

---

### Abiding in His Presence
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** From Visiting God to Dwelling with Him

Most Christians visit God; few actually live with Him. Move beyond spiritual commuting to discover what Jesus meant when He said "Remain in me" and experience a faith that never clocks out.

---

### Surrender to God's Will
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** The Freedom Found in Holy Release

Surrender is not defeat; it is the only path to victory. This series explores the counterintuitive truth that losing control is the only way to find peace.

---

### In the Beginning: The God Who...
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Encountering the Creator in Genesis

Before history began, there was God. Return to Genesis to meet the God who creates, speaks, separates, and blesses, and discover how these ancient words still shape your today.

---

### What is the Gospel?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** The Story That Changes Everything

The Gospel is not a ticket to heaven; it is a complete reordering of reality. Strip away religious assumptions to encounter the announcement that turned the ancient world upside down.

---

### Why Jesus?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** The Scandal of Particularity

Among all the religious teachers and moral leaders, why this carpenter from Nazareth? This series confronts the audacious claims of Christ and invites you to decide what you will do with Him.

---

### What Does It Mean to Believe?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Beyond Mental Assent

Belief in the biblical sense is not agreeing to a set of facts. Discover the Hebrew and Greek concepts that reveal a faith that transforms not just your mind, but your entire existence.

---

### What is Carrying a Cross?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** The Cost of True Discipleship

Jesus never promised an easy life; He promised His presence in a difficult one. Unpack what it truly means to deny yourself, take up your cross, and follow Him daily.

---

### Once Saved, Always Saved?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Security, Assurance, and Perseverance

Can you lose your salvation? This series navigates one of Christianity's most debated questions with Scripture, wisdom, and pastoral care, leading you to confident rest in God's keeping power.

---

### What Happens When You Repeatedly Sin?
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Grace, Consequences, and Restoration

Sin has consequences, but God has character. This series explores the tension between persistent failure and persistent grace, offering neither cheap comfort nor crushing condemnation.

---

### The Nature of Belief
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Faith as Living Reality

Belief is not static; it is a living, breathing relationship. Explore how genuine faith grows, wavers, deepens, and ultimately anchors your soul in the unchanging God.

---

### The Work of God
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** What God Requires, What God Provides

Jesus said, "This is the work of God: to believe in the one He sent." What does it mean that faith itself is God's work in us? Discover the beautiful paradox of grace.

---

### The Blueprint of Community
**Duration:** 5 days | **Theme:** Learning from the First Church

The early church turned the world upside down with a radical vision of shared life. Study the Book of Acts to discover principles of community that still have power to transform today.

---

### Signs, Boldness, Opposition, and Integrity
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Marks of an Authentic Church

What does genuine Spirit-filled ministry look like? Through the lens of Acts, examine the marks that distinguished the early church from mere religion.

---

### Witness Under Pressure and Expansion
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** The Church Under Fire

Persecution did not destroy the early church; it spread it. Learn from believers who faced pressure and discover how opposition can become a catalyst for gospel advance.

---

### The Word Before Words
**Duration:** 5 days | **Theme:** John's Cosmic Prologue

"In the beginning was the Word." John opens his Gospel with a thunderclap of theology. Dive deep into the prologue that reveals Jesus as the eternal Word through whom all things exist.

---

### Genesis: Two Stories of Creation
**Duration:** 6 days | **Theme:** Understanding Genesis 1 and 2

Genesis opens with not one, but two creation accounts. Rather than contradiction, discover complementary perspectives that reveal the full wonder of God's creative work.

---

## Planned Future Series

### The Psalms: Songs for Every Season
**Duration:** 14 days | **Level:** All Levels

The Psalms are not polished prayers; they are raw, honest, and deeply human. Walk through the full emotional range of the human experience with the songbook Jesus Himself used.

---

### Spiritual Warfare: The Battle You Cannot See
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** Intermediate

The enemy is real, but he is not equal to God. Learn to recognize, resist, and overcome the spiritual forces that war against your soul without either obsession or denial.

---

### The Sermon on the Mount: Kingdom Living
**Duration:** 21 days | **Level:** Intermediate

Jesus' most famous teaching is also His most demanding. Three weeks walking through Matthew 5-7 will redefine what it means to be His disciple in a world that opposes His ways.

---

### Parables: Stories That Shatter Assumptions
**Duration:** 10 days | **Level:** All Levels

Jesus told stories that his enemies hated and his followers rarely understood. Enter the subversive world of the parables to have your assumptions about God, self, and neighbor lovingly demolished.

---

### Romans: The Cathedral of Christian Doctrine
**Duration:** 30 days | **Level:** Advanced

Paul's letter to Rome is the most comprehensive statement of Christian theology ever written. This month-long journey will stretch your mind and transform your understanding of grace.

---

### Marriage and Singleness: Holy Callings
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** All Levels

Whether married or single, your relational status is a calling, not a waiting room. Discover the unique purposes and challenges God has for you in the life He has given you.

---

### Grief and Loss: Mourning with Hope
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** All Levels

Christians grieve, but not as those who have no hope. This tender series meets you in sorrow and walks with you toward the God who catches every tear.

---

### Anxiety and Fear: Finding Peace
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** All Levels

Jesus commanded "fear not" more than any other imperative. Explore what makes fear unnecessary when you know the One who holds tomorrow.

---

### Money and Generosity: Kingdom Economics
**Duration:** 7 days | **Level:** All Levels

Jesus talked about money more than almost any other topic. Discover why, and learn the freedom that comes when you hold your resources with open hands.

---

### The Names of God: Who He Reveals Himself to Be
**Duration:** 14 days | **Level:** Beginner

Jehovah Jireh, El Shaddai, Adonai. God reveals His character through His names. Learn them, and you will never see Him the same way again.

---

## Category Series Summaries

### For New Believers
Series designed for those just beginning their faith journey, building essential foundations with accessible language and practical application.

### For Spiritual Growth
Series that deepen existing faith, introducing spiritual disciplines, biblical literacy, and mature theological reflection.

### For Crisis Seasons
Series that meet believers in specific difficult circumstances: grief, anxiety, trials, doubt, and persistent sin.

### For Theological Depth
Series that explore complex doctrines and challenging passages for believers ready to grow in understanding.

### For Practical Living
Series that apply faith to everyday life: work, relationships, money, and decision-making.

---

*Voice note: All descriptions maintain EUONGELION's sacred, calm, deep, inviting tone. They speak to real human need without being preachy or corporate. Each description answers the unspoken question: "Why should I spend my time on this?"*
