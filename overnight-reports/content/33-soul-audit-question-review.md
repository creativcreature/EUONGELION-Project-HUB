# EUONGELION Soul Audit Question Review

*Review of existing questions with improvements, gap analysis, and new question proposals*

---

## Overview of Current Soul Audit

The Soul Audit currently has **24 questions** across **8 categories**:
- Faith Foundation (3 questions)
- Prayer Life (3 questions)
- Scripture Engagement (3 questions)
- Community (3 questions)
- Service (3 questions)
- Spiritual Disciplines (2 questions)
- Heart Condition (3 questions)
- Life Integration (4 questions)

---

## Category-by-Category Review

### 1. Faith Foundation

**Current Questions:**
1. "I have a clear understanding of who God is and His character." (Jeremiah 9:23-24)
2. "I am confident in my salvation and relationship with Jesus Christ." (1 John 5:13)
3. "I regularly experience God's presence in my daily life." (Psalm 16:11)

**Assessment:** Good foundation. Questions cover knowledge, assurance, and experience.

**Improvement Suggestions:**

| Current | Improved | Rationale |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Q1 | "I can articulate who God is based on Scripture, not just feelings." | More specific about SOURCE of understanding |
| Q3 | "I practice awareness of God's presence throughout my ordinary daily activities." | Emphasizes "practice" as active, "ordinary" normalizes expectation |

**Proposed Additional Question:**
> "I understand why Jesus had to die and what His resurrection accomplished."
> - *Category:* faith_foundation
> - *Scripture:* Romans 5:8-11
> - *Rationale:* Tests understanding of gospel core, not just assent to it

---

### 2. Prayer Life

**Current Questions:**
1. "How often do you spend dedicated time in prayer?" (1 Thess 5:17)
2. "I feel comfortable talking to God about anything." (Hebrews 4:16)
3. "I regularly pray for others beyond my immediate needs." (Eph 6:18)

**Assessment:** Covers frequency, intimacy, and intercession. Missing listening component.

**Improvement Suggestions:**

| Current | Improved | Rationale |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Q1 | "I intentionally set aside undistracted time for prayer each day." | Quality and intentionality, not just frequency |

**Proposed Additional Questions:**
> "I spend time listening for God's voice in prayer, not just speaking."
> - *Category:* prayer_life
> - *Scripture:* 1 Samuel 3:10
> - *Rationale:* Addresses the common imbalance of one-way prayer

> "My prayers include confession, gratitude, and adoration, not just requests."
> - *Category:* prayer_life
> - *Scripture:* Psalm 100:4
> - *Rationale:* Tests breadth of prayer life beyond petition

## Gap Analysis

### Categories Missing or Underrepresented

1. **Suffering and Hardship Response**
   - No questions about how faith holds up in trials
   - Proposed category: "Trials and Trust" or expand "Heart Condition"

2. **Biblical Worldview**
   - No questions about how faith shapes understanding of world events, culture, ethics
   - Could be added to "Life Integration"

3. **Spiritual Warfare Awareness**
   - No questions about awareness of spiritual opposition
   - Could be added to "Faith Foundation" or new category

4. **Rest and Sabbath (beyond discipline)**
   - Only one question on sabbath as discipline
   - Need questions about actual rest and margin in life

5. **Digital Life**
   - No questions addressing technology, social media, screen time
   - Critical for contemporary relevance

## Question Type Recommendations

### Current Types Used
- scale (1-10)
- frequency (1-10)

### Recommended Additions

1. **Binary Clarity Questions**
   - "Have you shared your faith with someone in the last month?" (yes/no)
   - More concrete than scales for action items

2. **Open Reflection Questions** (optional, for engagement)
   - "What is one area where you feel God is working on you right now?"
   - Not scored, but valuable for user reflection

3. **Scenario-Based Questions**
   - "When you receive unexpected money, your first thought is typically to: give, save, spend, or pray about it"
   - Tests actual behavior rather than ideal belief

## User Experience Recommendations

1. **Progress Indicator**
   - Show category progress ("Scripture Engagement: 2 of 4")
   - Reduces abandonment from feeling endless

2. **Scripture Display**
   - Show scripture reference after answering, not before
   - Reduces "right answer" bias

3. **Randomize Within Categories**
   - Questions in different order each time
   - Reduces pattern recognition bias

4. **Save and Continue**
   - Allow users to pause and return
   - Especially important if questions increase

5. **Retake Option**
   - Encourage quarterly or semi-annual retakes
   - Show progress over time
