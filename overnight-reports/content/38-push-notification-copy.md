# EUONGELION Push Notification Copy

*30 daily devotional reminder variations to avoid repetition*

---

## Notification Philosophy

EUONGELION notifications should:
1. Invite, never guilt
2. Be brief (under 100 characters ideal)
3. Vary enough to stay fresh
4. Reflect the sacred, calm tone
5. Never create anxiety about "missing" something

## Silent Hours Recommendation

**Do not send notifications:**
- Before 7:00 AM (user local time)
- After 9:00 PM (user local time)
- Unless user has set specific preference

**Ideal send times:**
- Morning users: 6:30-7:30 AM
- Midday users: 12:00-1:00 PM
- Evening users: 6:00-7:00 PM

## Tone Guidelines

### Do:
- Keep it brief
- Invite, don't demand
- Use calm language
- Vary phrasing over time
- Respect user's time

### Don't:
- Use urgency language ("Don't miss!")
- Create guilt ("You haven't read today")
- Include streak counts
- Use excessive emojis
- Send more than once per day

### Voice Comparisons:

| Avoid | Prefer |
|-------|--------|
| "Don't break your streak!" | "Your devotional is ready." |
| "You haven't read today!" | "A word for today awaits." |
| "Open now!" | "When you're ready." |
| "You're missing out!" | "An invitation awaits." |
| "3 days until your streak resets!" | (Don't send this at all) |
