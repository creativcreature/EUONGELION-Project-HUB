## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do:
- Speak with calm confidence
- Acknowledge where users actually are
- Offer invitation, not obligation
- Use Scripture naturally, not as proof-texts
- Respect the user's time and intelligence

### Don't:
- Use guilt or shame
- Be preachy or churchy
- Use Christian jargon without explanation
- Promise what we can't deliver
- Create artificial urgency

### Sample Voice Comparisons:

| Avoid | Prefer |
|-------|--------|
| "Don't break your streak!" | "Come back when you're ready." |
| "Unlock achievement: Bible Reader!" | "You've been showing up. Keep going." |
| "Share with friends to earn rewards!" | "Found something meaningful? Share it." |
| "You've been away too long!" | "Welcome back. Let's begin again." |
| "Complete your profile for full access!" | "A free account saves your journey." |

---

*Voice note: Onboarding should feel like entering a quiet sanctuary, not signing up for a gym membership. Every screen should lower anxiety and increase invitation. The goal is to make the user feel welcomed, not sold to.*
